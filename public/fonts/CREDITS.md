# Font credits

## CziKareChodz3

Polish-diacritics extension of **ChiKareGo2** by **Giles Booth**, used for
buttons, inputs, form labels and window title bars.

- Original: ChiKareGo2 — © Giles Booth
- License: **Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY)** —
  https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/
- Modifications: added Polish diacritics (ą ć ę ł ń ó ś ź ż + capitals), drawn
  on the font's 64-unit pixel grid; renamed to CziKareChodz3. See
  `scripts/fonts/chikarego2-polish.py`.

## Czikago

Polish-diacritics extension of **ChicagoFLF** by **Robin Casady**, a revival of
the original Macintosh **Chicago** by **Susan Kare**. Used for headings, the
calendar and the cursor chat.

- ChicagoFLF is distributed as **public domain** (Robin Casady).
- Modifications: added Polish diacritics, reshaped acute, renamed to Czikago.
  See `scripts/fonts/add-polish-glyphs.py`.
